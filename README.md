# 💜 HERsafe - Women Safety & Wellness App

HERsafe is a Flutter-based women safety and wellness application designed to provide quick emergency assistance, location sharing, and personal health tracking features in one simple and user-friendly platform.

The app helps users stay connected with trusted contacts during emergencies while also managing their menstrual health through a built-in period tracker.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🚨 Emergency SOS

- One-tap SOS emergency button
- Quickly send emergency alerts
- Share current location with trusted contacts
- Fast access during dangerous situations

---

## 📍 Location Tracking

- Real-time user location tracking
- Location sharing with emergency contacts
- GPS-based emergency assistance
- Helps trusted people locate the user quickly

---

## 📞 Emergency Contacts

- Add trusted contacts
- Manage emergency numbers
- Send alerts instantly during emergencies

---

## 🩸 Period Tracker

- Track menstrual cycles
- Record period dates
- Predict upcoming periods
- Maintain personal health records

---

## 💬 Chat Support

- Simple in-app chat feature
- Provides basic guidance and support
- Easy communication within the application

---
